Cloudflare Cookies

The site uses cookies provided by Cloudflare for the following purposes:

  • Bot Protection - necessary cookies used to protect the site from automated attacks and ensure security.
  • Traffic Analysis - optional cookies used to analyze how users use the site.

Cloudflare may store the following cookies:

  • __cf_bm - cookie used to distinguish between humans and bots.
  • __cflb - cookie used to manage traffic.
  • __cfruid - cookie used to limit the number of requests to the site.

Cookie Management

The user has the right to decide whether to accept all cookies, only necessary ones, or to reject them. Most browsers allow you to control cookies through browser settings.
